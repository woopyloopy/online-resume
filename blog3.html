5 Super Things For Building New Habits in Just 30 Days

Develop new hobby is hard, ironically we usually write a bucket list of resolution at the beginning of the year which a half of people not consistent about what they wrote.

Doing it for a couple of month or even worse just a couple of weeks doesn’t makes us be a pro in one things. Hobbies can make us easier to achieve goals in the future, based on its interest.

These 5 simple things can help you to build new hobby, here we go:

Create new identity

Our habits are created from our behaviour. Your identity from people put on you or maybe you, use that to stop building new habits and breaking bad habits. For example, people say that Putra is too lazy, he thinks to start new habits that is going to gym once a week. He can’t stand by his feet and always hear other’s opinion about him, so he decides to cancelling all the plans.

No matter how page you read, you are a reader, and no matter how words you write you are a writer. When you think your identity has change you start to act according to your new identity.

Stack new habits

On daily basis you can stack your new habits to previous habits, just like you start new habits to read at least 4 page of book in the morning, you can place your habits before you are going outside example this step by step:

Wake up – make your bed – shower – breakfast - read a book – go to work

Make it easy

Prepare all things that is easy to reach and see. If you want to make reading a book as your routine, you can keep your book open and easy to see otherwise put your smartphone away from you. That can causes distraction, what social media do in 15 minutes? Nothing. Better waste your time wisely.

Give reward

A little achievement of your habits can makes you feel satisfied. Really! It feels like your effort is giving result. For example, after finish read one book once a week you can transfer your money to your saving account. It gives you some motivation to do more because we love to achieve a rewards.

Track your habits

There is a lot of fun app at digital store you can use for tracking daily habits. As human we tends to forget doing things and sometimes be a procrastinator. It’s satisfying to make a lot of streak on a certain habits in a funny way.

Are you ready to be a pro in your habits and gain benefit from them? Now or never! Good luck!
