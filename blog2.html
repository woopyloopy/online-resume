Why Data is Essential for Developing New Technology

Wherever field of industry we almost certainly have heard a great story about how data is changing the face of our world. It might be an important part of process to achieve final goal just like make a digital product more efficient and analysing target market action through ads.

Data in general is a collection of facts including numbers, words, measurements, observations,etc that has been translated into a form that computers can process.

Simply we called data with information. In tech company especially if it’s about big data, data refers to information that is machine-readable as opposed to human-readable.

Human-readable also known as unstructured data that means only human can understand and intrepret such an image or the meaning of a block of text otherwise machine-readable or unstructured data means information that computer programs can process. Programmer set instruction while build program for manipulating data, that data must have kind of uniform structure to perform instruction on data.

There is many of data that company can use to build their technology more suitable for users but these are three more common :

Personal data

Anything spesific from users that covers demographics, location,email address and other identifying factors. Remember? When sign up into mobile app or website, the app or web always asking about our personal information. Not only from web and app, social media sites also gather your information in detailed about your habits and interests.

Company use your personal data to keep you engaged with personalized suggestions. Facebook, and instagram uses your personal information to suggest content you might like to see based on what other people similar to what you like.

Mostly your personal data is used for advertising and competitive research purposes that’s why after you search something for example you search cat food at facebook search then you see some ads that sell what you have been looking for. You already get tergeted ads and content from companies you’ve never heard.

Transactional data

Have you ever make a purchase from clicking some ads? The company use your data as their conversion from spreading ads in several social media like facebook ads or instagram ads

This is called transactional data which requires an action to collect. Every website you visit collects transactional data that can’t record your activity on the website like google analytic, another 3rd party system or their own internal data capture system.

Benefit of this information is help to expose variablity and optimize operations for the highest quality results and create competitive advantages and result in business like more effective marketing and increased revenue.

Web data

Anything you can find on the web that is public (not stored in internal database). This data can be very informative especially when communicate to management. Web data has function to track potential customers, monitor competitors, build apps, and many more.

Data become as big data means simply represents increasing amount and the varied types of data being gathered as part of data collection. Differentiates from regular data is the tools used to collect, store and analyze had to change to acommodate the increase in size and complexity. No more sampling, all datasets can be process and gain more complete results.
