No more interaction with robot, Here is UX Writer comes to you!

“404 not found”

*the things go skrrrttt*

Jeez, what does it mean? Like come on i’m not get into deep in technology and system so, what should i do now?

I really feel your pain, sometimes we’re getting into problem while using digital product that we don’t even know what happened with the machine, it can happens anytime while you are using things e.g smartphone, tablet, and desktop. You are not tech savvy person and you still need technology things to make your job more easier and faster. But it still makes confusion:( Okay, calm. All problem has its solution, as always.

Here is it, user experience writer. Maybe you are new to know this roles. What they do in daily basis? They work to make you happy everyday! Just to make sure you’re not close the app after getting some error message and not make your head will explode like *boom shakalaka* calm down user, we get you cover!

User is the first thing important in developing digital product like mobile app, desktop app, and website, without users who will use your product? No one. That makes company start the running process or we will called it with design-sprint for their user enjoy using the product. No time wasting anymore *whooshh*.

What background roles that fit to be an UX writer? as far as I know, any background can be UX writer but the important things you must skilled in writing skill and can humanize digital product to be friendly with users.

I have an unique experience with two popular e-commerce. I get confused and worried in checkout process. At “S” e-commerce they did’t write detailed voucher description and the term condition as a result it makes me confuse why I can’t use this voucher anymore and can’t change the shipping system to COD. At “L” e-commerce when I get back after cancelling to checkout, the system make the checkout process to finish. How can it happens? :( as a new user I feel afraid really. So, the prevent action to make this things never happened is to create great user experience based on user research, and a/b testing.

Copywriting and content writing aren’t same as UX writing. It’s totally different, from their purpose to principle. In UX writing we know 3 principle that must implementing in copy, there are clear, concise, and usefull. The less do more. UX writer work to help user achieve their goal in the app. UX writer are important to create copy in the app and work closely with UX team include designer. They are get involve in the design process from the very beginning.

From Lisa Sanchez at medium, yeah the same name as me. She said that:

“UX writing is the practice of designing the words people see when they interact with software. It’s about designing the conversation between a product and its user.”

Create copy that jargon free and explain clearly, then we can have more accessible content, and inclusive to a wider audience. By using simple language, the product experience will improve for all users include your grandma if the segmentation of the app is for every generation.

Everything from the button with text are from UX writer and every piece text in the app is their job. The crafting word process is not only compelling and concise but also user-friendly, appropriate with context, and on-brand. On-brand means that the copy is adaptation from company brand voice not only implemented in the app but also at email newsletter, social media, website, etc.
